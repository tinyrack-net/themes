## Contract

Use `Root/Trigger/Portal/Positioner/Popup` for the surface and compose `Item` or `Separator` inside it. A nested menu uses `SubmenuRoot` and `SubmenuTrigger` around its own `Portal/Positioner/Popup` surface.

## API

`Root` owns controlled or uncontrolled open state, orientation, modality, looping focus, and dismissal. `Trigger` opens the menu; `createHandle` connects a detached trigger and typed payload to a root.

`Portal`, `Backdrop`, `Positioner`, `Popup`, `Arrow`, and optional `Viewport` compose the collision-aware layer. `Group`, `GroupLabel`, and `Separator` organize its content. Use `Item` for commands and `LinkItem` for navigation; links remain open unless `closeOnClick` is set.

`CheckboxItem` with `CheckboxItemIndicator` represents independent choices. `RadioGroup` contains `RadioItem` and `RadioItemIndicator` for one-of-many choices. `SubmenuRoot` and `SubmenuTrigger` connect a nested `Portal` / `Positioner` / `Popup`. Base UI owns roving focus, typeahead, keyboard activation, disabled-item skipping, portal positioning, and outside/Escape dismissal.
