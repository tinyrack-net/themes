## Choose the hierarchy

Start with the reading order, then choose the smallest role that expresses it. Use `display` for rare page-level statements, heading roles for nested sections, `body` or `bodySm` for reading, `caption` for supporting detail, and `label` for short identifiers.

## Line length and localization

Keep paragraphs narrow enough to scan and let labels grow. Korean and Japanese may wrap at different points from English; do not reserve width from the English string or force explanatory text onto one line. Use uppercase tracking only for short Latin labels, not Korean or Japanese prose.

## Font loading responsibility

Core declares the family name `"IBM Plex Sans"` for body, heading, and mono tokens. It does not ship or load font files. The consuming app must load the required faces and locale subsets; this documentation app loads IBM Plex Sans plus its Korean and Japanese families.

## Implementation and reference

```css
@import "@tinyrack/ui/core.css";
.rack-title { font: var(--tinyrack-weight-heading) var(--tinyrack-text-2xl) / var(--tinyrack-leading-sm) var(--tinyrack-font-heading); }
```
